# Morrigan Aensland

The succubus queen and face of the Darkstalkers series. A well-rounded character
with strong air control, projectile game, and versatile normals. One of Capcom's
most recognizable characters outside of Street Fighter.

## Archetype

Morrigan is effectively a **shoto with flight**. Soul Fist + Shadow Blade mirrors
Hadoken + Shoryuken, but her air mobility and flight options give her a
unique mid-range and pressure game.
