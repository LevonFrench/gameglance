# Chun-Li

The First Lady of Fighting Games. An Interpol officer and one of the most
iconic characters in the genre. Known for her strong normals, fast movement,
and varied kick-based special moves.

## Cross-Game Identity

- Fastest walk speed in many games
- Strongest standing normals (st.HP, st.HK are often her best buttons)
- Charge character in some games, motion in others
- In SF6: Serenity Stream stance adds unique follow-ups
