# Akuma (Gouki)

The master of the Satsui no Hado. A dark, powered-up variant of the [[shoto]]
archetype. Akuma trades health for devastating damage and versatile offense.
Classic glass-cannon design across every game he appears in.

## Design Philosophy

Akuma's defining trait across all games: **high damage, low health**. He hits
harder than [[ryu]] and [[ken]] but dies faster. His air fireball and demon flip
give him angles of attack the other shotos lack, making him a more complex and
rewarding character at the cost of survivability.
