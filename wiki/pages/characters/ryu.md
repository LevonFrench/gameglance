# Ryu

The quintessential fighting game character. A wandering martial artist seeking
the true meaning of the fight. Ryu defines the [[shoto]] archetype — balanced
gameplay built around the hadoken, shoryuken, and tatsumaki.

## Archetype

**[[shoto]]** — the foundational archetype. Balanced between offense and defense.
Good at all ranges but doesn't dominate any single area. Ryu is the measuring
stick against which all other characters are compared.

## Evolution Across Games

- **SF2 / Super Turbo**: The original. Straightforward gameplan — fireball zoning,
  anti-air with shoryuken, punish with combos.
- **SFα3**: Gained ISM system options. V-ISM custom combos added combo creativity.
- **CvS2**: Groove selection adds variety. A-Groove Ryu with custom combos, C-Groove
  with lvl2 cancel. Evil Ryu variant is glass cannon.
- **SF6**: Denjin charge mechanic adds new layer — charged fireballs for stun.
  Drive System gives access to Drive Rush cancels, Drive Impact, etc.
