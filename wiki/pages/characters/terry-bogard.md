# Terry Bogard

SNK's flagship fighting game character. The Legendary Hungry Wolf. Protagonist
of the Fatal Fury series and a staple of SNK crossovers. Terry is an
all-rounder with strong fundamentals, good projectile game, and a powerful
reversal.

## Cross-Game Identity

Terry maintains a consistent identity across all appearances:
- Always has Power Wave (or similar ground projectile)
- Always has Burn Knuckle
- Buster Wolf and Power Geyser as supers
- "Are you okay?!" remains iconic across every game
