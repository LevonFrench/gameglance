# Ken Masters

Ryu's rival and training partner. While [[ryu]] embodies balanced fundamentals,
Ken favors an aggressive, rushdown-oriented variant of the [[shoto]] archetype.
His Shoryuken is traditionally fire-infused and stronger, while his Hadoken
tends to be weaker.

## SF6-Specific Mechanics

Ken's SF6 iteration introduced several unique mechanics:

- **Quick Dash** — a forward dash that can be cancelled into specials
- **Jinrai Kick** — a multi-hit special with follow-up options
- **Dragonlash Kick** — an aerial approach tool
- **Run into specials** — Quick Dash → Shoryuken/Tatsu/Dragonlash pathways

## Archetype

**[[shoto]] / rushdown hybrid**. Ken leans more aggressive than Ryu across all
games. In SF6 especially, the Quick Dash system gives him relentless pressure
that distinguishes him from Ryu's more zoning-oriented gameplan.
