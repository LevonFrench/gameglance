# Fatal Fury: City of the Wolves

SNK's return to the Fatal Fury series, released 2025. Features the REV System
and a mix of returning Fatal Fury / Garou characters alongside guest fighters.

## Core Mechanics

- **REV System** — see [[rev-system]] (page pending)
- **S.P.G. (Strategic Precision Gauge)** — meter management
- **Guest Characters** — Chun-Li, Ken Masters, Cristiano Ronaldo, Salvatore Ganacci

## Notable Features

- First mainline Fatal Fury game since Garou: Mark of the Wolves (1999)
- Heavy Garou: MotW legacy in the returning cast (Rock, Terry, B. Jenet, etc.)
- Cross-company guest characters from Street Fighter
