# Capcom Fighting Jam

Capcom's internal crossover, released 2004. Pulls characters from Street Fighter II,
Street Fighter III, Street Fighter Alpha, Darkstalkers, Star Gladiator, and
Red Earth, each retaining their home-game mechanics.

## Roster (20 characters)

Ryu, Guile, Zangief (SF2 reps), Alex, Yun, Urien (SF3 reps), Guy, Karin,
Sakura, Rose (SFα reps), Felicia, Demitri, Jedah, Anakaris (Darkstalkers reps),
Nool (Hydron), Hauzer (Red Earth reps), Mukuro (Kenji), Leo (Star Gladiator reps),
Ingrid, Pyron (originals/bosses).

## Move Categories

**Normal Moves**, **Special Moves**, **Super Arts**.
