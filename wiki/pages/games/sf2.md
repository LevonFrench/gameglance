# Super Street Fighter II Turbo

The definitive version of Street Fighter II, released 1994. Introduced super combos
and Akuma as a hidden boss. One of the most competitively enduring fighting games
ever made.

## Roster (17 characters)

Ryu, Ken, E.Honda, Chun-Li, Blanka, Zangief, Guile, Dhalsim, T.Hawk, Cammy,
Fei Long, Dee Jay, Balrog, Vega, Sagat, M.Bison, Akuma.

## Move Categories

**Normal Moves**, **Special Moves**, **Super Arts**.

## Core Mechanics

- **Super Combo Gauge** — build meter for level 3 super
- **Old Characters** — input code to play previous versions (no super)
- **Akuma** — hidden boss character, banned in most tournaments
