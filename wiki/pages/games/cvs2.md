from both companies and the Groove system for playstyle customization.

## Move Categories

The app organizes moves into: **Special Moves**, **Super Arts**, **Normal Moves**.

## Core Mechanics

- **Groove System** — 6 grooves (C, A, P, S, N, K) each with different meter,
  guard, and movement options
- **Roll Cancel** — famous glitch/technique, depends on groove
- **Just Defend** — SNK-style parry (K-Groove)
- **Parry** — SF3-style parry (P-Groove)
