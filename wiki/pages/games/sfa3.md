# Street Fighter Alpha 3

Capcom's expanded Alpha fighter, released 1998. Features the ISM system
(X-ISM, A-ISM, V-ISM) for playstyle selection.

## Roster (34 characters)

Ryu, Rolento, Dan, Blanka, Sakura, Birdie, Charlie, Akuma, Bison, Dhalsim,
Vega, Adon, Cody, Cammy, Zangief, T. Hawk, Rose, Guy, Honda, Juni, Gen,
Chun-Li, Fei-Long, Karin, Juli, Balrog, Sodom, Sagat, Ken, Dee Jay, R. Mika,
Guile, Evil Ryu, Shin Akuma.

## Move Categories

**Normal Moves**, **Special Moves**, **Super Arts**.

## Core Mechanics

- **ISM System** — X-ISM (simple, powerful), A-ISM (balanced), V-ISM (custom combos)
- **V-ISM Custom Combos** — defining mechanic of the game
- **Guard Crush** — break opponent's guard with pressure
